About the Game

Rocket Rush is an arcade game inspired by the classics of the 80s. The goal is simple: guide your spaceship through space and survive the obstacles. Plus, there's a versus mode where you can compete against another player to see who survives the longest.

With energetic music and effects that heighten the adrenaline, Rocket Rush will transport you back to your childhood memories while challenging you to beat your own time!

Game Features

Arcade Mode

Survive as long as possible while dodging asteroids and other space traps.

VS Mode

Face off against another player to see who can survive the longest in this skillful showdown.

Adrenaline-Pumping Music

An energetic soundtrack to keep you alert and raise the intensity of every game.

Retro Style

With graphics and effects that will remind you of your childhood retro games, but with a challenging gameplay twist.
